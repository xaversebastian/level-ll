# LevelEleven — TestFlight

## What is LevelEleven?

LevelEleven is a harm-reduction companion app for substance use. It helps you track intoxication levels, stay informed about risks, and look out for yourself and your friends — all in real time.

This app does **not** encourage drug use. It provides tools for people who choose to use substances to do so more safely and with better awareness.

---

## Key Features

### Home — Real-Time Level Tracking
- Log doses and see your current intoxication level on an 11-segment gauge
- Personalized calculations based on your body weight, tolerance, and medications
- Timeline view showing how your level changes over time
- Active warnings when risky thresholds or interactions are detected

### Care — Aftercare & Wellbeing
- In-session tips tailored to the substances you're currently using
- Normalization guidance to help you understand what you're feeling
- Post-session aftercare reminders and wellbeing check-ins over the following days
- Content adapts to your experience level — more detail for beginners, concise info for experienced users

### Group (Session Mode) — Track Together
- Start a live group session and add friends from saved profiles
- See everyone's intoxication level at a glance
- Log past sessions retroactively with substance selection and feedback
- Session history with detailed archives

### SOS — Emergency Help
- Quick access to emergency guidance for yourself or someone else
- Glasgow Coma Scale (GCS) calculator to assess consciousness
- Overdose recognition checklist
- One-tap emergency call button with localized numbers (112 / 911 / 999)

### Drug Combinations — Interactive Safety Matrix
- Visual combination chart for all tracked substances
- Color-coded risk levels: from low-risk synergy to dangerous
- Tap any combination for a detailed explanation of risks and interactions
- Based on established harm-reduction data (TripSit)

### More — Info & Settings
- Detailed substance information (pharmacokinetics, risks, safer use)
- Harm reduction basics and drug checking service info
- Profile management, data export, and app settings
- Review the onboarding walkthrough at any time

## What to Look For During Testing

- **Stability:** Does the app crash or freeze at any point?
- **Data:** Are doses, sessions, and profiles saved and loaded correctly?
- **Layout:** Does everything look right on your device and in both light/dark mode?
- **Onboarding:** Is the flow clear and does it guide you through setup smoothly?
- **Accuracy:** Do intoxication levels, warnings, and timelines seem reasonable?
- **Usability:** Is anything confusing, hard to find, or frustrating to use?

Please report any bugs, visual glitches, or suggestions via TestFlight feedback. Screenshots are always helpful.
